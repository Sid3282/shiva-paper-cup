About Us

Shiva Paper Cup International Pvt. Ltd, is the leading manufacturer of high-quality disposable paper products in Nepal. We are committed to sustainability and innovation.

Our products include paper cups, takeaway cups, paper plates, and aluminum containers, all designed to meet the highest standards of quality and environmental responsibility.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, sustainable, and eco-friendly disposable products to our customers.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's leading manufacturer and supplier of disposable paper products, promoting environmental responsibility in every product we create.
